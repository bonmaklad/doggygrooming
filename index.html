Meet Cut & Cuddle Dog Groomers - Kerikeri

Welcome to Cut & Cuddle Dog Groomers, a professional dog grooming service based near Kerikeri. At Cut & Cuddle, we are committed to delivering high-quality grooming in a calm, safe, and caring environment. Every dog is treated with patience, kindness, and respect, with grooming services tailored to suit their individual needs, coat type, and temperament. Our priority is ensuring your dog feels comfortable and relaxed throughout the grooming process while achieving reliable, professional results. Whether your dog requires a simple tidy-up or a complete full groom, you can be confident they will receive attentive care from start to finish. For added convenience, pick-up and drop-off services can be arranged upon request, making it easier for busy owners to keep their dogs well groomed. If you are seeking professional and dependable dog grooming services in Kerikeri and the surrounding areas, Cut & Cuddle Dog Groomers would be delighted to care for your dog.
